Automotive Ads

  • CONTEXT

    Creative Intern at Constellation

  • TIMELINE

    May 2024 - Aug 2024

  • SKILLS

Background

As a Creative Intern at Constellation, I designed digital ads across multiple automotive clients simultaneously, supporting campaign creative for a wide range of brands.


Challenge

Deliver production-ready ads quickly while staying fully compliant—every asset needed accurate model details, readable legal disclaimers, and strict adherence to each brand's guidelines.


My Approach

  • Cross-team collaboration: worked with Customer Success and Quality Control to ensure compliant designs
  • Multi-format production: adapted creative across display, motion, video, and “Everything Ads”
  • Brand system switching: matched each brand's typography, color, imagery, and tone
  • QA + iteration: turned feedback quickly, tracked versions cleanly, and delivered launch-ready assets
