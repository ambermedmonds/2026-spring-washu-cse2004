About Me

I'm a designer who loves turning complex problems into intuitive, human-centered digital experiences. As a senior at Washington University in St. Louis studying Marketing with minors in Design and Business Analytics, I approach design with both creative empathy and analytical thinking.

With experience across product management, marketing, and creative roles, I think beyond individual screens by considering user behavior, business goals, and technical constraints when designing solutions. I'm especially interested in how thoughtful design decisions can drive engagement, solve real problems, and translate complexity into clear, user-friendly interfaces.

When I'm not designing, you can usually find me on the tennis court, exploring a new city, or behind a camera.
