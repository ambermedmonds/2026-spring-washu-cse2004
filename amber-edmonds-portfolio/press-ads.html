Henry Rose Press Ads

Context Creative Intern at Constellation
Timeline May 2024 - August 2024
Skills Graphic Design

Background

During my internship at Constellation as a Creative Intern, I designed digital ads for one of our clients Henry Rose. The goal was to translate third-party press coverage into social-ready creative that felt editorial, premium, and on-brand.


Challenge

Condense long-form reviews into short, scroll-stopping headlines, while keeping the layouts clean, credible, and visually consistent across multiple ad variations.


Objective

Create a cohesive set of ad concepts that highlight Henry Rose's press validation through attention-grabbing quotes, strong hierarchy, and polished product-focused visuals.
