About Roamly

Roamly is a grad-trip planner designed to turn “we should go somewhere” into an actual plan. Senior spring gets busy fast, so we keep things lightweight enough to get everyone on the same page before finals, interviews, and post-grad schedules take over.

Roamly gives you several ways to get started. You can browse featured destinations for highlights, sample itineraries, and key trip details. Or, if you're tired of making decisions and want to speed up the process, take the Trip Quiz to get recommendations based on your group's vibe, budget, and travel style. Once you've narrowed things down to a few options, use the comparison table to line up options side-by-side and see what fits best.

Our approach is simple: plan enough to make it happen, then leave room for the good stuff. Choose a home base, commit to at least one "must-do" experience, and leave the rest open so the best parts can happen on their own.
